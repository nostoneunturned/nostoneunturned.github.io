No Stone Unturned SRD

No Stone Unturned is a Tabletop RPG about exploring a world long after a calamity. This site contains the full rules, which are broken into 4 main parts. Grab the links below or on the sidebar. This is a living document which will be updated with changes to the rules until the final version is published. Thanks for reading!

The .pdf can be purchased on itch.io. Additionally, we will be crowdfunding the printing of a zine containing the full rules as a part of Zine Quest in February 2020.

Feel free to remix these rules and make whole new games out of them. All I ask is that you let me know about your project so I can check it out and that you reference No Stone Unturned somewhere in the document as an inspiration.
If you are using the "Details" system, that is based on the Indie Hack, so refer to their SRD for attribution requirements.
